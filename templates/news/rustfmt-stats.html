Date: 2024.04.03

In the past week after several false direction I made some progress collecting information about the use of rustfmt. I found that 14,951 of the crates (10.53%) have rustfmt.toml and 5,420 crates (3.81%) have .rustfmt.toml 35 crates have both. As far as I understand rustfmt will disregard rustfmt.toml.

BTW there are 11 crates without an owner. Is that a bug in Rust-digger or in the database of Crates.io or my ow understanding of the world?

Number of crates

There are now 141,982 (1,773 more than a week ago).

The array_width field has a lot of very different values. There are crates where it is set to 0 or 1 or 500000.
